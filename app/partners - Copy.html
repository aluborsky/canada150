Partners

The following organizations are partners of the Canada 150 project. They have committed to creating their own unique projects to help Canadians record their short and full-length family and community histories as well as municipal and corporate histories. Check out their websites to learn more about these organizations.

Founding Partners

Canada's History is Canada’s premier history site for students, teachers, history buffs, history professors and anyone interested in learning about, or participating in, the history of Canada. It has a robust website chalk full of resources, as well a magazine for adults and one for children. They work with the Governor General of Canada to honour our best history teachers annually. Canada’s History is a fundamental partner in Canada 150 and has been for many years.

Canadian Science & Technology Museums Corporation is responsible for preserving and protecting Canada’s scientific and technological heritage and promoting and sharing knowledge about that heritage. The Corporation and its three Museums — the Canada Agriculture Museum, the Canada Aviation and Space Museum, and the Canada Science and Technology Museum — tell the stories of Canadian ingenuity and achievement in science and technology, and demonstrate how these accomplishments have contributed to the building of our country.
