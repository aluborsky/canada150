Legal Deposit Form

Print off and fill in the form below for each book you donate to Library and Archives Canada, as required by law. If the form does not print on one page, right click on the form and choose the option: “Open image in new window.” This opens the form up on a clean page and should allow you to print it on one single page.

Fill out the form and together with two copies of your book, DVD or CD, mail the book to:
