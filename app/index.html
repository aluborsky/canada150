Our Canada, Our Stories is the largest, collaborative history-gathering project ever. It celebrates our country's 150th anniversary.

Upload a story with a photo/illustration and know that you, your family and friends, and their descendants can find it, search through it and enjoy it forever. The legacy of your stories will be an invaluable gift to Canada on its 150th anniversary.

See other people’s stories and photos and add your own personal, family and community stories as your gift to Canada on its 150th anniversary. Also send us a picture (JPG, GIF or PNG files). Your stories and photos will be open to the public, searchable, retrievable and saved forever.
