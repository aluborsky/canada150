Our Canada, Our Stories is the largest, collaborative history-gathering project ever. It celebrates our country's 150th anniversary. Upload a story with a photo/illustration and know that you, your family and friends, and their descendants can find it, search through it and enjoy it for ever. The legacy of your stories will be an invaluable gift to Canada on its 150th anniversary.
